Series Highlights

Shared strengths across the series — built for integration, usability, and consistent clinical viewing. Designed as a medical-grade display for reliable operation in clinical environments.

I/O

Versatile Connection

Supports both conventional analog and digital signal input/output, allowing seamless integration with various operating room equipment and enabling daisy-chain connection of multiple displays for simultaneous visualization.

HD

Full-HD / High-Resolution LCD Panel

Delivers high resolution, high contrast, and wide viewing angles, enabling surgeons to observe medical images with enhanced precision and clarity.

D 

DICOM / Gamma Calibration

The compliance with DICOM/ GAMMA ensures optimized color and gray scale reproduction for best image accuracy.

Optimizes color rendition for superior color accuracy and enhances grayscale consistency, ensuring precise medical image presentation and reducing the risk of diagnostic misinterpretation.
